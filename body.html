Greetings from Shaastra, IIT Madras!

The excitement for Shaastra 2018 is at an all-time high! With about just two weeks to take the leap, we want you to be aware of formal procedures to be a part of Shaastra.

It is mandatory for all participants and visitors to have a SHAASTRA ID & SHAASTRA Passport. Your Shaastra ID is {{festID}}. Please make a note of it and include it in all communications.

Shaastra Passport is a document that gives exclusive access to and contains information all the events, workshops, lectures, shows, exhibitions, games, helpline numbers and prestigious IIT Madras research lab visit, this year. The passport is essential to participate in all events of Shaastra and it is mandatory for all participants to buy, in order to get access to Shaastra 2018.

Follow these steps to obtain your passport:

  1. After creating a Shaastra account and receiving a Shaastra ID, go to userprofile page and pay ₹ 250. To collect your physical copy of your passport, visit the registration desk at KV Grounds inside IIT Madras to collect your passport by showing your ID and verification by student coordinators.
  2. For on-ground passport sales, there will be a separate registration desk at KV Grounds for on-spot participants during Shaastra. You can pay ₹250 and collect your passport. But we request you to pay online to avoid hassles and queues during Shaastra.
Please Note:
  1. All participants in a team must have individual Shaastra IDs and should have separate passports to participate in the event.
  2. All free workshops at Shaastra also require all individual participants to compulsorily have Shaastra Passport.
